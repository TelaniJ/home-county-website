Top activities to do in Nairobi

Nightlife

Nairobi boasts a lively nightlife scene with numerous bars, clubs, and lounges. Enjoy stunning views of the city skyline while sipping cocktails and experiencing the local arts scene by performing artists.

Safari

This includes game drives, walking trails, picnic spots, wildlife visits, ziplining, hiking, historical exhibits, tandem jumps, scenic flights, team games, tactical challenges among others.

Cultural Experience

Immerse yourself in Kenyan culture and traditions in Nairobi. Enjoy live performances of traditional dances, music, rich delicacies, and storytelling from various ethnic groups.Learn about Kenya's rich history, culture, and natural heritage featuring exhibits on archaeology, ethnography, paleontology, and contemporary art.

Fine Shawries

Meet a variety of fine ladies from different ethnic groups, confident in their being. They are also known for being very intelligent and are placed in variety of professional fields. Beauty with brains!
